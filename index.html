01 — About

A little background

I'm a full-stack developer with a B.S. in Computer Science from East Texas A&M University (minor in Mathematics) and over 5 years of professional experience building production-grade eCommerce and backend systems.

At Traxxas I wear two hats: hands-on developer and Scrum Master. Day to day that means architecting Magento 2 modules and RabbitMQ publisher/consumer pipelines, integrating with Oracle APEX and MSSQL, and running agile ceremonies for my team. I enjoy problems that sit at the intersection of data architecture and real-time reliability — getting disparate systems to stay in sync automatically.

Before Traxxas I was at Innovation First International, where I contributed to a Magento 1 → 2 migration, integrated Algolia Instant Search, built Laravel applications, and developed a Vue.js power consumption calculator for Rack Solutions that helped customers estimate a server rack's energy draw and cooling needs before purchase.

I've also incorporated Spec-Driven AI Development into my workflow — defining system architecture up front before writing a line of code, which has meaningfully improved delivery speed and reduced rework.
